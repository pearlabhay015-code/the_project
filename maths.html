Department of
Mathematics

The Department of Mathematics is supported by DST-FIST for the year 2024-2029. The department is a partner under INDO-NORWEGIAN CO-OPERATION PROGRAMME 2024 (INCP-2) with partner University of Bergen, Norway. The Department offers two-year M.A./M.Sc. programme (NEP 2020), 5-year integrated UG-PG programme in Mathematics (NEP 2020), and Ph.D. programme in Mathematics in diversified areas of Mathematics and Mathematical Sciences. The department also offers a number of courses as elective to the students of the other departments. The Department also offers B.Sc. (Mathematics) courses to the 4-year integrated B.Sc.-B.Ed. programme. The Department provides a common computer laboratory equipped with all computational facilities.

The B.Sc. (Hons.) Mathematics programme provides a rigorous foundation in mathematical concepts, theories, and applications. Students are introduced to modern fields like algebra, analysis, geometry, and applied mathematics. The programme follows NEP 2020 with multiple exit options and credit-based flexibility.

The M.Sc. Mathematics programme at CUSB is designed for graduates who wish to deepen their understanding of advanced mathematical concepts, theories, and applications. The programme emphasizes independent research culminating in a dissertation project in the final semester, equipping students for careers in research, academia, and industry.
