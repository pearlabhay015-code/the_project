Hostel Facilities

Separate, well-maintained hostel blocks for boys and girls — with 24/7 security, mess, and recreational amenities.

Girls Hostel — Gargi Sadan & Maitreyi Sadan

Gargi Sadan

Maitreyi Sadan

Girls Hostel

The hostel administration takes all care to provide quality accommodation to the students so that they may feel at home. The rooms are well-furnished with basic amenities for a comfortable living. There are separate blocks for girls and boys with all essential facilities. The mess building is well organised with advanced kitchen equipment to provide quality services.

— Infrastructure & Amenities —

• Fully furnished rooms (sharing basis) — wooden bed, wardrobe, study table & chair
• Mess Facilities & RO-purified Drinking Water
• Common washrooms, Reading Room, Visitors' Room, Medical Room
• Recreational Room with Television & Gymnasium/Fitness Centre
• Geysers, Fire Protection System, Safe assembly points
• 24×7 Medical Assistance & Ambulance Service
• Wi-Fi connectivity, CCTV Coverage & 24×7 Electricity/Water supply
• Caretakers, Housekeeping, Salon facilities, ATM (within 100 m), Canteen

— Sports Infrastructure —

• Table Tennis, Badminton Court, Basketball Court (Synthetic), Volleyball Court
• Hockey ground, Football ground, Handball ground, Athletic track
