About the University

Collective Reasoning,
National Purpose

Central University of South Bihar (CUSB) is one among 54 Central Universities under the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India. Established under the Central Universities Act, 2009 (Section 25) as Central University of Bihar (CUB), it was renamed by the Central Universities (Amendment) Act, 2014.

The University operates from a permanent 300-acre campus at Panchanpur, approximately 15 km from Gaya — in the historic heartland of Bihar, the land of enlightenment.

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in teaching, research, and innovation — contributing to national development and global knowledge creation.

Mission

To foster an inclusive academic environment promoting critical thinking, ethical values, and interdisciplinary research.

Motto

Collective Reasoning — fostering collaborative inquiry and shared wisdom across all disciplines and communities.

NEP 2020

Aligned with National Education Policy 2020 — promoting holistic development, skill enhancement, and multidisciplinary learning.

Infrastructure

Campus & Facilities

World-class infrastructure across 300 acres — built to support every dimension of academic and student life.

Library Services

Digital and physical collection with access to thousands of journals, e-resources, and research databases.
Hostel Facility

Separate blocks for men and women with 24/7 security, mess facility, and recreational spaces.
Sports Complex

Cricket, football, athletics grounds, and a modern indoor sports hall for students and staff.
Auditorium & Conference Hall

State-of-the-art venues for seminars, conclaves, cultural programs, and annual convocations.
Smart Classrooms & Wi-Fi

Fully Wi-Fi enabled campus, smart classrooms, computer labs, and a dedicated media production studio.
Biodiversity Park

Green park featuring medicinal plants, Dhanvantari Arogya Vatika, and ecological trails across the campus.
Health Care Centre

On-campus centre providing medical assistance, emergency care, and wellness support for students and staff.
Central Instrumental Facility

Advanced research instruments available for interdisciplinary scientific research by faculty and students.
