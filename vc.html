Role as Vice-Chancellor
Responsibilities & Vision

As the 3rd Vice-Chancellor of CUSB, Prof. Singh has prioritised a congenial learning environment, efficient and transparent administration, and a strengthened student support system in line with NEP 2020. He emphasises professional courses suited to the 21st century and focuses on research rooted in the principle of Integral Humanism.

  • Chief executive and academic head of the university
  • Implementing NEP 2020 across all departments
  • Strengthening research and development culture
  • Promoting 'Vocal for Local' and AatmaNirbhar Bharat ideals
🔬
Research & Contributions
Academic & Social Impact

Prof. Singh conducted joint research on urban fringe studies of Indian and German cities at Ruhr University, Bochum. He is the founder General Secretary of the Institute for Rural Eco-development and has been organising the Prithvi Parva National Seminar every year for 25+ years — generating policy recommendations for planners and decision-makers.

  • DAAD-sponsored research at Ruhr University, Germany
  • Executive Editor — 'Journal of Eco Development'
  • President, Deccan Geographical Society of India, Pune
  • Senate Member, IIT Patna
