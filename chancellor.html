Role at CUSB
Responsibilities & Governance

The Chancellor is the ceremonial head of the Central University of South Bihar, appointed by the President of India (who serves as Visitor of all Central Universities). While executive powers vest with the Vice-Chancellor, the Chancellor plays a vital role in governance oversight and institutional prestige.

  • Presides over all Convocation ceremonies
  • Chairs important University Court meetings
  • Provides strategic guidance and vision
  • Represents CUSB in national academic forums
🔬
Medical & Scientific Contributions
Research & Public Health

Dr. Thakur is internationally renowned for his pioneering work on Kala-azar (Visceral Leishmaniasis / Black Fever) — a life-threatening tropical disease endemic to Bihar and parts of South Asia. His research significantly shaped treatment protocols adopted globally.

  • Treatment protocols for Kala-azar adopted by WHO
  • Published ~300 research articles in medical & clinical sciences
  • Recipient of WHO Lifetime Achievement Award (2017)
  • Expertise in Tropical Diseases & Public Health
